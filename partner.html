Who can be a Monexo Partner?

These are the ones who can become a Monexo Partner

  • Independent Chartered Accountants/CA Firms

    Accountants who are not associated with firms but are licensed or otherwise authorized to practice accounting by a state or jurisdiction.

  • Corporate Deposit Distributors/ Post Office Deposit Distributors

    An individual or a company which are distributing NBFC Deposits or Corporate Deposits or Post Office Deposits. With Monexo you can enable your clients to earn better.

  • Mutual Fund Distributors

    Individuals or organizations that facilitate investors' purchases and sales of mutual fund units.

  • Insurance Agents

    An Individual or a firm who have clients who they interact regularly to advice on various insurance products.

  • Wealth Managers

    Individuals who specialize in advising high-net-worth individuals and affluent families on how to invest their portfolios and plan their finances for success

  • Financial Consultants

    Individuals whose job is to give people and companies advice about investing their money, getting loans, etc

Escrow Account

We are the first P2P company in India to partner and hold your funds in an escrow account. We have partnered up with IDBI Trusteeship Services Limited as our third-party administrator. This makes the whole process a lot easier because they can execute lending-related instructions given by you.

Borrower Screening

All our borrowers are from India. We collect essential documents to verify their background and assess their credit quality to determine an interest rate appropriate to their probability of repayment.

How Do you become a Monexo Partner?

  • Register

    Register online as a partner on our app. Our team will reach out for the next steps.

  • Share & Monitor

    You will get a unique link using which you can refer it to your clients and also you get access to a dashboard through which you will be able to track your client’s investments.

  • Get Paid

    You get paid for every successful invesment made. Payouts are done on a monthly basis.
